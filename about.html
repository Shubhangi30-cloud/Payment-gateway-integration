We are an organisation settled in Singapore, operating globally which helps students in making their dreams comes true. We provide them with mentorship, internships, workshops, scholarships and help them grow in their respective fields. We prepare students to be ready for what lies ahead in their respective careers. This time around we decided upon starting a donation drive which focuses solely on helping the stray dogs of our society, providing them with food, shelter, and help them get adopted. We built ourselves from the scratch and now we need your support. Come on and join in hands with us to help us feed the strays and provide them with a forever home.
